Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Effective Date: February 19, 2026 · Last Updated: February 19, 2026

1. Scope

This Privacy & Policy Statement describes how Bowie Research Group ("BRG," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when you visit our website, contact us, subscribe to updates, register for events, or otherwise interact with BRG online (the "Site").

This Statement does not govern services delivered under a separate written agreement with BRG. If you are a client under contract, that agreement may include additional confidentiality and data-handling terms.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

A. Information You Provide Directly

  • Contact details (name, email, phone number, organization, role)
  • Inquiry details (message content, project needs, attachments)
  • Event/Program information (registration details, participation details)
  • Recruiting information (resume/CV, work history, and related details) if you apply or submit interest

B. Information Collected Automatically

  • Device and usage data (IP address, browser type, device identifiers, pages viewed, time spent, referring pages)
  • Approximate location derived from IP address
  • Cookies and similar technologies (see Section 5)

C. Information from Third Parties

If you interact with BRG pages on third-party platforms (e.g., LinkedIn), we may receive limited information as permitted by those platforms and your privacy settings.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Respond to requests, questions, and proposals
  • Provide Site functionality and improve performance and content
  • Administer programs, events, and communications you request
  • Conduct recruiting or participant intake where applicable
  • Analyze Site traffic and usage trends
  • Maintain security, prevent fraud, and enforce our policies
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect BRG's rights

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers who support our operations (e.g., website hosting, analytics, email delivery, form tools), under contractual obligations appropriate to their role
  • Professional advisors (legal, accounting, insurance) as needed
  • Legal and safety purposes (to comply with law, respond to lawful requests, protect rights/safety, investigate misuse)
  • Business transfers (e.g., merger, acquisition, reorganization), where permitted by law

5. Cookies and Analytics

We may use cookies, pixels, and similar technologies to operate the Site and understand usage. These tools may collect information about your device and browsing activity.

Your choices: You can control cookies through browser settings and, where implemented, on-site cookie preference tools. Disabling certain cookies may reduce Site functionality.

6. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfill the purposes described above, including to respond to requests, maintain records, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

7. Data Security

We maintain administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; therefore, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights regarding your personal information (such as access, correction, deletion, or opting out of certain processing). You may also opt out of marketing emails by using the unsubscribe link included in those emails.

To request access or exercise applicable rights, contact us using the information in Section 12.

9. Children's Privacy

The Site is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. External Links

11. Important Notice About Information on This Site

BRG may publish commentary, research summaries, and analysis for informational purposes. Nothing on the Site should be interpreted as legal advice, financial advice, investment advice, or a guarantee of outcomes. Decisions made based on Site content are your responsibility.
