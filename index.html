The Pattern We Keep Seeing

Most businesses we meet have the same structural problem: the operation runs on memory.

The quoting process lives in someone's head. The data is "close enough" but requires constant manual correction. Documentation exists in three different systems, or doesn't exist at all. When the key person is out, everything slows down.

That looks like normal growing pains until volume increases, a key employee leaves, or you try to sell the company. Then the cost of tribal knowledge becomes very real, very fast.

How We Work

Discovery → Build → Handoff

01

Discover

We start by figuring out what's actually broken, not what you think is broken. We ask questions, audit systems, and map the gap between how things should work and how they actually work.

02

Build

We design and implement the systems your business needs: process documentation, data governance, workflow automation, ERP configuration. We use AI tools internally to work faster, but the deliverable is always a system your team can run.

03

Hand Off

When we leave, you own everything. Documented, trained, and operational. No open-ended retainers. No dependency model. The goal is a system that works without us in the room.

Is This a Fit?

You're a good fit if

Your business has outgrown the spreadsheets and memory that got you here. You have real operational complexity (custom products, configured orders, multi-step processes) but the systems haven't caught up. You might be preparing for growth, preparing for an exit, or just tired of being the single point of failure. You're willing to invest in building something real, not a band-aid.

Probably not a fit if

You need cheap hourly labor, want someone to run your operations indefinitely, or are looking for a pre-packaged solution dropped in without discovery.
